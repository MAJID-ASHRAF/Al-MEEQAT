Rakaat Counter

If you are late for the prayer and the Jama’at has already begun, you wonder which rakaat is going on. You join the prayer and try to figure out how many rakaats you missed. In such a case, won't it help you if the display shows you the exact number of rakaats that are over? That's precisely what our Rakaat counter does. And it is very useful to count rakaats automatically in Taraweeh in Ramazan.
